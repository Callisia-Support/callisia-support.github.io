Privacy Policy — Callisia

This Privacy Policy describes how Callisia (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information related to its games, products, and associated services. It details what data we collect, the legal bases for processing, who we share it with, how it is used, how long it may be retained, and how you can exercise your privacy rights.

Last updated: March 26, 2026 Controller: Callisia

This policy is drafted to comply with the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA/CPRA (California/USA), and LGPD (Brazil).

1. Who We Are?

Callisia is an indie studio focused on game development, aiming to create fun, creative, and personality-driven experiences. As part of our work, we also develop applications with an emphasis on clarity, objectivity, and functionality. In everything we do, we value performance, transparency, and respect for the user in every detail.

For the purposes of data protection laws, we act as the Data Controller of the personal data you provide directly to us or that is collected through our applications.

2. What Data We Collect

We do not require mandatory registration and do not collect direct identifying information (such as real name, social security numbers, phone, address, documents, or photos) without your voluntary contact. Nevertheless, our products integrate third-party services that may collect or receive certain data automatically.

2.1 Data You May Provide Directly

  • Technical Support: Username or email provided when submitting a query, as well as communication history.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically (via Google AdMob & Firebase)

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and specific app and device IDs.
  • Analytics Data: Interactions with the game, session time, performance data, crashes, diagnostics, and similar metrics (Crashlytics).
  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system, language, app version, network type, and similar technical information necessary for operation, measurement, and security.
  • Approximate Geolocation Data: IP address (used to determine country or city, never the exact location).

Important Note: Callisia does not store any data directly on its own database; automatic data collection occurs in accordance with app settings, user consent, and applicable law.

3. How We Use Information (Legal Bases)

In accordance with GDPR and LGPD, we only process data when we have a legal basis:

  • Contract Performance: To allow you to play and access app features.
  • Consent: To display personalized advertising, measure campaign performance, and prevent fraud, according to your jurisdiction.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve your experience, analyze usage trends, improve technical performance, and ensure app security.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, tax requirements, regulatory obligations, and valid requests from authorities.

4. Data Sharing and "Sale"

We do not sell your personal data for money. However, we share device identifiers with advertising partners (Google AdMob). Under CCPA (California), sharing for targeted advertising may be interpreted as "selling" or "sharing."

  • Technical Partners: Google LLC (Firebase and Analytics) for infrastructure, security, and performance analysis.
  • Advertising Partners: AdMob for monetization (following your opt-out preferences).
  • Public Authorities: When required by law or when necessary to protect rights, security, and service integrity.

    • Note: When sharing occurs for advertising or measurement purposes, it will be done according to user consent and/or applicable jurisdiction rules.

5. Advertising, Consent, and Analytics

You can manage how your data is used for advertising directly in the application settings or through the consent form (CMP) that appears when starting the app in regions such as the EU.

5.1 Operational Partner Policies

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display ads and measure their performance. Depending on the country and user settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized, and the processing involved follows the Google AdMob privacy policy.
  • Firebase and Analytics: We use Firebase and analytics features to understand app usage, improve performance, and diagnose problems, as per the Firebase privacy and security policy.

5.2 Google Play Services

Some features of Android and the Google ecosystem itself may depend on Google Play services. When this occurs, the related processing is governed by the Google privacy policy and official support information from Google Play services.

6. Children and Minors

Our applications are mostly intended for the general public. We comply with COPPA (USA) and Article 8 of the GDPR. We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13 (or 16 in the EU) beyond what is strictly necessary for the operation of the app and integrated services. If we detect that a child's data has been collected without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

Where required by law, we will take steps to handle ads and data collection in a manner appropriate for minors, including the use of age-appropriate ad settings and limiting personalization when necessary.

7. Your Rights (Detailed Section)

Regardless of where you live, Callisia guarantees the following rights:

7.1 For Residents in the European Union (GDPR) and Brazil (LGPD)

  • Access and Portability: Obtain a copy of the data in a structured format.
  • Rectification: Correct incomplete or inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request the definitive deletion of your records.
  • Objection and Restriction: Deny the use of data for marketing purposes or legitimate interest.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw previously given permissions at any time.

7.2 For Residents in the USA (CCPA/CPRA - California and Others)

  • Right to Know: What categories of personal data we collect and who we share it with.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Request that we do not "sell" or "share" your data for targeted advertising.
  • Right Over Data: Right to access information held about you, correct inaccurate data, and delete data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not be punitive (e.g., limiting game access) if you exercise your privacy rights.

To exercise any applicable right, send an email to callisia.support@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days (GDPR) or 15 days (LGPD).

8. International Data Transfers

As we use global third-party services, your information may be processed in other countries, including countries outside your country of residence. This may include servers (primarily in the USA via Google Cloud), measurement tools, and Google infrastructure.

When applicable law requires specific safeguards for international transfer, we will adopt appropriate mechanisms to protect the data.

9. Security and Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy (e.g., while the app is installed), address requests, resolve disputes, maintain records required by law, and operate the service. Data processed by Google AdMob and Firebase is also subject to the policies and settings of those services and may be kept for longer periods for historical performance analysis.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When there is a significant change, we will publish the updated version with the new revision date. Continued use of the game after the update indicates that you have read and understood the latest version.
